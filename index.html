Hi, i'm Kiran, nice to meet you.

I work in IT and been working in IT Support for 7 year. Graduated in 2013 with a degree in Software Engineering, I was hired as a software analyst, working with internal bespoke systems. After a year and half I found my self working for a defence company working in IT support, supporting the business. My plan was to go into Networks working within the company and complete CCNA. But since the lock down, it has created an opportunity to revisit what I wanted to do long before completing University. Now I have signed up to a online courses and started to work on small porjects to familiarise myself with the new technology in web development. I now what to move into a career in web development.
